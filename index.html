FAQ

Do you provide free estimates?

Yes. We’ll review the repair, talk through options, and provide a written scope with clear pricing.

Can you help with both residential and commercial repairs?

Absolutely. We keep homes running smoothly and handle light commercial needs like storefront fixes, rental turnovers, and office maintenance.

How do you price projects?

All repairs receive a fixed price. You will be informed if the project has issues that could impact the fixed price, and we'll discuss other options or future projects to resolve the issue.

What areas do you serve?

Mount Pleasant, Scottdale, Greensburg, Hempfield Township, Irwin, Unity Township, Jeannette, Latrobe, Everson, and the surrounding communities in Southwestern PA.
